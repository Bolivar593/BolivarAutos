Our story

Rooted in this community for over 50 years

Wild Strawberry Children's Centre began in 1971 as a group of parents who wanted quality, welcoming care for their children in Winnipeg's West End. What started as a small parent cooperative has grown into a trusted community centre spanning multiple locations.

We embrace inclusion and celebrate the diversity of the families we serve — a place where every child, and every family, belongs.

Under the leadership of Executive Director Amrita Premdas, who has guided the centre since 2010, Wild Strawberry has grown while staying true to its cooperative roots — bringing consistency, warmth and a deep commitment to early childhood education to every family who walks through our doors.
